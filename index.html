About

A designer who thinks like a marketer.

I’m drawn to the point where a well-made object meets a well-told story. My industrial design training gives me the craft (sketching, CAD, prototyping and finish) while my marketing degree means I’m always asking who it’s for and why they’d care.

That combination is rare, and it’s what I bring to every brief: the ability to design the thing and the reason people choose it.
