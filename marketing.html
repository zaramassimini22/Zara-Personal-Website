03 · What I do

Three jobs,
one mission.

Growth through acquisition only works if clients never feel the seams. My work concentrates on the three things that make a multi-site firm feel like one.

A

Brand rollout & rebranding

Bringing acquired firms (Watson Erskine, Baskin Clarke, Canny Group and Evolve) under a single Wilson Pateras identity across the website, signage, stationery and collateral, so every location reads as one brand.

B

Acquisition integration

Smoothing the transition for newly acquired practices, coordinating client-facing communications and web content so existing clients feel informed, reassured and retained through the change.

C

Events & launches

Supporting launch moments for new offices, from on-site branding to community-facing marketing that introduces each location to its local area.

04 · How it works

The rollout playbook.

Step 01

Audit & align

Map each incoming practice’s existing branding and touchpoints, then identify everything that needs to shift to the Wilson Pateras system.

Step 02

Rebrand & equip

Apply the brand across the website, signage, print and client collateral so the new office looks established, not transitional.

Step 03

Launch & introduce

Publish the new location online and support its public launch with events and local marketing that welcome clients and community.
